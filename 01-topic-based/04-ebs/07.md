## One extra tip for the SAA exam

AWS loves combining services in one question. This one is a perfect example:

* **Compute:** EC2
* **Block storage:** EBS
* **Object storage:** S3
* **Archival:** S3 Glacier Flexible Retrieval
* **Backup:** EBS Snapshots (mentioned only as a distractor)

Train yourself to identify each requirement separately instead of looking for one service that does everything. That's often the fastest way to eliminate wrong answers.
